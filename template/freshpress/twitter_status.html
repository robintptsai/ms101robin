Notice:
  • This function makes you be able to share your article to Twitter.
  • Your blog has associated with @#{twitter.name}.
  • You can disassociate your Twitter account from your blog via Twitter Connections at any time.
  • You can also click here to associate again.
  • Your blog hasn't associated with a Twitter account, you need click here to associate.
  • You haven't set the Twitter consumer key and secret. To enable this function, you need to register an app at Twitter.
  • When you registering, please set Application Type to "Browser", Callback URL to "#{{TWITTER_CALLBACK_URL}}", Default Access type to "Read & Write".
  • After you get the key and secret, please set TWITTER_CONSUMER_KEY and TWITTER_CONSUMER_SECRET to them in the setting.py file.
