Notice:
  • Delete category will empty the category property of its articles. You can move its articles to other category before this operation.
  • If it contains sub categories, you need handle them first.
  • This operation may take much time, so please don't change this category or its articles until you receive a mail that notices the task has been finished.
  • If you don't receive notification email for a long time, you can login to the Admin Console to check the status of Task Queues, and delete it if needed.
